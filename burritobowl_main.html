What should I put in a burrito bowl?

All kinds of things!

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole
  • Peppers
  • Rice
  • And more!

You can put anything in a burrito bowl, really. Typically, the basis for a burrito bowl consists of rice, taco filling, beans, and corn (which this tutorial will teach you how to do).
