What kind of mushrooms should I buy?

The most commonly purchased and consumed kind of mushrooms in the US are white (or button) mushrooms, but many other varieties exist and are eaten across the US. For example:

  • Chanterelle
  • Oyster
  • Portobello
  • Shiitake
  • Cremini
  • And more

Mushrooms are also commonly found in vegan dishes as a way to improve texture, such as in "lentil loafs" to give the dish a meatier texture. They are also commonly used in ramen.
