What kind of tofu should I get?

It depends! There's a few different kinds of tofu:
  • Extra firm: A firm tofu that is generally used in things like stir fries or curries.
  • Silken tofu: An almost gelatinous tofu that is used in various recipes, from desserts to sauces.
  • Sprouted tofu: Similar to firm tofu, but has slightly better nutritional value and higher calorie count
Keep in mind that when preparing tofu, you'll have to press out some water to get the texture that you want. Another piece of advice, especially for firm tofu, is that you should freeze it for a couple of days and thaw before using. This gives the tofu a much firmer texture.
